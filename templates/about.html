About CORAZON

Welcome to CORAZON, an innovative web platform dedicated to predicting heart disease using advanced machine learning techniques. Our goal is to provide users with a convenient and reliable tool to assess their risk of heart disease based on their medical reports.

At CORAZON, we understand the significance of early detection and prevention when it comes to heart disease. By leveraging the power of machine learning, specifically the Random Forest algorithm, we have developed a robust predictive model that analyzes various health indicators to accurately assess the presence of heart disease.

How It Works

  • Accurate Predictions: Our Random Forest model has been trained on a comprehensive dataset and fine-tuned to ensure high accuracy in predicting heart disease. We have rigorously validated the model's performance to instill confidence in its results.
  • Privacy and Security: We prioritize the privacy and security of your personal health information. Your data is handled with the utmost care and in compliance with industry standards and regulations. We maintain strict confidentiality to protect your sensitive information.
